About RadiFace

Created by students, for students. We understand the unique challenges of maintaining healthy skin during university life.

Our Story

RadiFace was born from personal experience. As university students, we struggled with inconsistent skincare routines, breakouts during exam periods, and finding affordable products that actually worked.

After months of research into dermatology, behavioral psychology, and student lifestyle patterns, we realized that existing skincare apps weren't designed for our reality - irregular schedules, tight budgets, shared bathrooms, and high stress levels.

So we built RadiFace: an AI-powered skincare companion that understands student life and adapts to your unique circumstances, helping you maintain healthy skin without breaking the bank or your schedule.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us to create better skincare solutions for students

Accessibility

Skincare should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget, experience level, or living situation. We focus on affordable, effective solutions.

Evidence-Based

Every recommendation is backed by dermatological research and real student data. No trends, no gimmicks - just proven science.

Holistic Wellness

We believe skincare is connected to overall well-being. Our approach considers sleep, stress, mental health, and lifestyle factors.

Inclusivity

RadiFace is designed for all genders, skin types, and cultural backgrounds. We celebrate diversity and create solutions that work for everyone.

Simplicity

Complex routines don't work for busy students. We focus on simple, effective habits that fit seamlessly into your daily life.

Continuous Learning

We're constantly learning from our users and the latest research to improve our recommendations and user experience.
