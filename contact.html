Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about RadiFace

When will RadiFace be available?

We're currently in beta testing with select universities. The full app launch is planned for early 2025. Join our waitlist to be notified when it's available!

How much will RadiFace cost?

RadiFace will be free for basic features, with a premium subscription ($4.99/month) for advanced AI recommendations and detailed progress tracking. Student discounts will be available.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share personal data with third parties. Your photos and information are stored securely and you can delete your data at any time.
