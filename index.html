About RadiFace

We understand that university life is hectic. Between classes, assignments, part-time jobs, and social activities, maintaining a consistent skincare routine can feel impossible. That's why we created RadiFace.

RadiFace was created by a team of university students who struggled with inconsistent skincare routines. After researching dermatology and behavioral psychology, we built an app that considers the unique factors affecting student skin health - irregular sleep, exam stress, weather changes, limited budgets, and shared living spaces.

Gender-neutral and inclusive for all skin types
Budget-friendly recommendations under $50/month
Evidence-based skincare education and tips
Dorm-friendly and travel-sized product focus

Our Mission

To make skincare accessible, simple, and effective for every student, regardless of their experience level, budget, or living situation.
